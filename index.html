SAMPLING STIUDO

sampling is the reduction of a continuous-time signal to a discrete-time signal. A sampler is a subsystem or operation that extracts samples from a continuous signal.

The original signal can be reconstructed from a sequence of samples, up to the Nyquist limit, by passing the sequence of samples through a reconstruction filter.
